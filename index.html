jQuery Toggle Fields

Adds form conditions to show and hide form fields.

Initialisation

    
    toggleFields();

Example A

Toggling a single field

Selecting 'foo' from the select box will enable the field.

Example B

Toggling multiple fields at a time

Selecting 'Yes' will enable the fields.
